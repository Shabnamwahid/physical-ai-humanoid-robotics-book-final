Academic References​

Core Textbooks​

  • Siciliano, B., & Khatib, O. (2016). Springer Handbook of Robotics (2nd ed.). Springer.
  • Thrun, S., Burgard, W., & Fox, D. (2005). Probabilistic Robotics. MIT Press.
  • Russell, S., & Norvig, P. (2020). Artificial Intelligence: A Modern Approach (4th ed.). Pearson.
  • Dudek, G., & Jenkin, M. (2010). Computational Principles of Mobile Robotics (2nd ed.). Cambridge University Press.

Specialized Topics​

  • Pfeifer, R., & Bongard, J. (2006). How the Body Shapes the Way We Think. MIT Press.
  • Arkin, R. C. (1998). Behavior-Based Robotics. MIT Press.
  • Murphy, R. R. (2019). Introduction to AI Robotics (2nd ed.). MIT Press.
  • Spong, M. W., Hutchinson, S., & Vidyasagar, M. (2006). Robot Modeling and Control. Wiley.

Books and Publications​

Physical AI and Embodied Intelligence​

  • Pfeifer, R., & Scheier, C. (1999). Understanding Intelligence. MIT Press.
  • Clark, A. (2008). Supersizing the Mind: Embodiment, Action, and Cognitive Extension. Oxford University Press.
  • Wheeler, M. (2005). Reconstructing the Cognitive World: The Next Step. MIT Press.
  • Chemero, A. (2009). Radical Embodied Cognitive Science. MIT Press.

Humanoid Robotics​

  • Nakanishi, J., Cory, R., Mistry, M., Peters, J., & Schaal, S. (2008). Operational space control: A theoretical and empirical comparison. International Journal of Robotics Research.
  • Kajita, S. (2019). Introduction to Humanoid Robotics. Springer.
  • Ogata, T., & Musashi, T. (2016). Humanoid Robotics. Woodhead Publishing.

ROS and Middleware​

  • Quigley, M., Gerkey, B., & Smart, W. D. (2015). Programming Robots with ROS: A Practical Introduction to the Robot Operating System. O'Reilly Media.
  • Kammerl, J. (2012). Robot Operating System (ROS): The Complete Reference. Springer.
  • Joseph, A., & Quigley, M. (2017). ROS Robotics Projects. Packt Publishing.

Research Papers​

Foundational Papers​

  • Brooks, R. A. (1991). Intelligence without representation. Artificial Intelligence, 47(1-3), 139-159.
  • Brooks, R. A. (1986). A robust layered control system for a mobile robot. IEEE Journal on Robotics and Automation, 2(1), 14-23.
  • Pfeifer, R., & Scheier, C. (1999). Self-organization, embodiment, and biologically inspired robotics. Science, 280(5371), 1721-1723.
  • Steels, L. (1994). The artificial life roots of artificial intelligence. Artificial Life, 1(1), 75-111.

Recent Advances​

  • Chen, X., et al. (2023). A Generalist Robot Learning Model via Large-Scale Visuomotor Pre-training. arXiv preprint arXiv:2306.00954.
  • Brohan, C., et al. (2022). RT-1: Robotics Transformer for Real-World Control at Scale. arXiv preprint arXiv:2212.06817.
  • Ahn, M., et al. (2022). Do As I Can, Not As I Say: Grounding Language in Robotic Affordances. arXiv preprint arXiv:2204.01691.
  • Driess, D., et al. (2023). PaLM-E: An Embodied Multimodal Language Model. arXiv preprint arXiv:2303.03378.

Vision-Language-Action Integration​

  • Radford, A., et al. (2021). Learning Transferable Visual Models From Natural Language Supervision. arXiv preprint arXiv:2103.00020.
  • Li, J., et al. (2023). Blip-2: Bootstrapping Language-Image Pre-training with Frozen Image Encoders and Large Language Models. arXiv preprint arXiv:2301.12597.
  • Zeng, A., et al. (2022). Socratic Models: Composing Zero-Shot Multimodal Reasoning with Language. arXiv preprint arXiv:2207.07001.

Conversational Robotics​

  • Kory, S., & Breazeal, C. (2020). Effects of a Social Robot on the Quality of Life of Older Adults with Dementia. ACM Transactions on Human-Robot Interaction.
  • Tapus, A., et al. (2007). User personality matching with humanoid robot assistive behavior for older users. AAAI Spring Symposium.
  • Belpaeme, T., et al. (2018). Social robots for education: A review. Science Robotics, 3(21), eaat5954.
